Privacy / 隱私權

Privacy Policy

Codex Material Themes is a skills-only plugin. It does not operate a publisher-controlled server, require an account, or send theme selections to the publisher.

Effective date / 生效日期：2026-07-20

Plugin data / Plugin 資料

The plugin reads its bundled theme catalogue and preview assets, then produces recommendations and a codex-theme-v1: setting in your ChatGPT or Codex conversation.

Plugin 只會讀取內附的主題目錄與預覽資產，並在 ChatGPT 或 Codex 對話中產生建議及 codex-theme-v1: 設定。

  • No publisher account or separate sign-in / 不需發布者帳號或額外登入
  • No publisher-operated analytics or advertising / 無發布者營運的分析或廣告
  • No automatic modification of Codex settings / 不會自動修改 Codex 設定

Website preferences / 網站偏好

The public website uses browser local storage to remember language and font choices on your device. These preferences are not sent to the publisher by the site.

公開網站使用瀏覽器本機儲存空間記住語言與字型選擇；網站不會將這些偏好傳送給發布者。

You can remove them by clearing site data in your browser. 你可以透過瀏覽器清除網站資料。

Platform processing / 平台處理

Your use of ChatGPT, Codex, and the hosted website may be processed by their platform and hosting providers under their own terms and privacy policies. The publisher does not control those platform records.

ChatGPT、Codex 與網站託管服務可能依各自的條款與隱私政策處理技術資料；發布者無法控制這些平台紀錄。

Sharing and retention / 分享與保存

The publisher does not sell personal information and does not maintain a separate database of plugin prompts, generated settings, or website preferences.

發布者不販售個人資料，也不另行建立資料庫保存 Plugin 提示詞、產生的設定或網站偏好。

Changes / 政策變更

This policy may be updated when the plugin's capabilities or data practices change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

若 Plugin 功能或資料處理方式變更，本政策也會更新；頁面上方生效日期代表目前版本。

Contact / 聯絡

Questions about this policy can be sent to robin@bud4.net or filed through GitHub Issues.

如對本政策有疑問，請寄信至上述信箱，或透過 GitHub Issues 聯絡。
